Humanoid

Art Piece – Posted December 7, 2021

When I initially drew this piece, I was keen to make it perfect (straight lines, proportional features, consistent spacing, etc.). But when I transferred it to an SVG, I was unconcerned with a perfect form. Instead, I focused on preserving the piece as it was drawn (i.e. keeping its imperfections).

I prefer the original piece over the updated SVG, but only when the two are compared next to each other. When they’re separated, I prefer the updated version. It’s an odd phenomenon where I prefer imperfections in art only when its compared to other art. But odd feelings like this are what make us human.
