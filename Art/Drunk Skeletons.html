Drunk Skeletons

Art Piece – Posted December 8, 2021

While alcohol isn’t hallucinogenic, with enough “x”s its as good a trip as any – at least as far as you’ll be able to remember. This is by no means an expression of that trip, it’s an expression of what that trip can become – a colorful death (or, at least the world will be colorful, you may not be).
