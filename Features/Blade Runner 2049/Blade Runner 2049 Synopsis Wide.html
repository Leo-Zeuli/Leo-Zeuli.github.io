Blade Runner 2049: Implied World Building (Analysis)
Image: Warner Bros.

Implied world building, as it sounds, is world building not directly communicated, but implied to exist somewhere out there. ¶ Most often, you see implied world building in sequels when the world building of the previous films is implied.
