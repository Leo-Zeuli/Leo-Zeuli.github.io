Loki Has a Few Tricks Up Its Sleeve (First 4 Episodes Review)

While the character arcs are forced in the most Disney way, Loki manages to twist a predictable plot to mild results. If your invested in the MCU, you'll find its tropes everywhere; this is rare MCU project where a lack on context will probably increase your enjoyment as you'll less acustomed to the heavy use of tropes.
